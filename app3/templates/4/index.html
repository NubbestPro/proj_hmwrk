Banana Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of cinnamon
  • 1 cherry
  • 250ml milk
  • 3 ice cubes

Method:

  1. Peel the banana and add to a blender
  2. Add the milk, cinnamon and ice cubes to the blender
  3. Turn on the blender for 30 seconds
  4. Add the cherry on top
  5. Drink!
